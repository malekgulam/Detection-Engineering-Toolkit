Quality Score Formula

Quality score is a composite metric that rewards high detection rates, penalises false positives, and applies a severity bonus for rules covering more serious techniques. 

Quality Score = (Detection Rate × 0.50) − (FP Rate × 0.30) + (Severity Bonus × 0.20)

Severity Bonus:  LOW = 15 · MEDIUM = 40 · HIGH = 70 · CRITICAL = 90
Score range:     0 – 100 (capped)
